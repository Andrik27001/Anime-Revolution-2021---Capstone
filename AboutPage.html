ANIME REVOLUTION 2021

Anime Expo is an annual convention dedicated to Japanese culture and currently held at the Los Angeles Convention Center. Popular events include; the Lecture, Exhibition, and the Anime cosplay Contest. Many of the attendees cosplay while attending the convention, and join in the many gatherings for fans of different anime, manga, gaming and other genres.
Please contact us per Email for any further questions about Anime Revolution 2021! AnimeRevo2021@losangeles.org
