"Kon'nichiwa Anime Lovers!"

ANIME REVOLUTION 2021

Bringing fans and industry together for an annual celebration of Japanese pop culture. Join us in Downtown Los Angeles for our 30th Anniversary!

2021.10.15(THU) ~ 16(FRI)

@ National Auditorium of los Angeles, Art and more

Main Program

___________

Lecture

Listen to the story of different celebrities including Japan's top anime authors and their opinion about 2021s Anime.

Exhibition

Look at the making of the bests animes and mangas of the year and the different technologies used to develop them

Cosplay

Participate in the AR cosplay competition and display your creations to the crowd on the Anime Revolution's stage.

Last News

Catch up on the most anticipated news about anime, new releases, video games, and upcoming seasons!

Connect

Get opportunities to work with the biggest anime enterprises in the world by sharing your anime projects and ideas.

Featured Speakers

___________

Hiro Mashima

Japanese manga artist "Mangaka"

_____

His best selling work, Fairy Tail, published in the same magazine from 2006 to 2017, has become one of the best-selling manga series with over 72 million copies in print.

Eiichiro Oda

Japanese manga artist "Mangaka"

_____

Eiichiro Oda is a Japanese manga artist and the creator of the series One Piece. One Piece is both the best-selling manga and the best-selling comic series of all time, in turn making Oda one of the best-selling fiction authors.

Hajime Isayama

Japanese manga artist "Mangaka" and character designer

_____

Hajime Isayama is a Japanese manga artist. His first and currently ongoing series, Attack on Titan, has become one of the best-selling manga series of all time with 100 million copies in circulation as of December 2019.

Hideaki Anno

Japanese manga artist "Mangaka", animator, anime creator, director, screenwriter, actor, producer, designer and businessperson.

_____

Best known as the creative mastermind behind Neon Genesis Evangelion, Anno has plenty more strings to his bow, working in live-action and illustration as well as being a voice actor.

Hayao Miyazaki

Japanese manga artist "Mangaka", animator, director, producer, screenwriter and author.

_____

Co-founder of Studio Ghibli, a film and animation studio. He has attained international acclaim as a masterful storyteller and as a maker of animated feature films, and is widely regarded as one of the most accomplished filmmakers in the history of animation.

Masashi Kishimoto

Japanese manga artist "Mangaka"

_____

His best known work, Naruto, has sold over 250 million copies worldwide in 46 countries. Besides the Naruto manga, Kishimoto also personally supervised the two canonical anime films, and has written several one-shot stories.
