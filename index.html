Featured Speakers

Akira Toriyama

Japanese manga artist "Mangaka" and character designer

Akira Toriyama is a Japanese manga artist and character designer. Toriyama is regarded as one of the artists that changed the history of comics, as his works are highly influential and popular, particularly Dragon Ball, which many comics artists cite as a source of inspiration.

Eiichiro Oda

Japanese manga artist "Mangaka"

Eiichiro Oda is a Japanese manga artist and the creator of the series One Piece. One Piece is both the best-selling manga and the best-selling comic series of all time, in turn making Oda one of the best-selling fiction authors.

Hajime Isayama

Japanese manga artist "Mangaka" and character designer

Hajime Isayama is a Japanese manga artist. His first and currently ongoing series, Attack on Titan, has become one of the best-selling manga series of all time with 100 million copies in circulation as of December 2019.

Hideaki Anno

Japanese manga artist "Mangaka", animator, anime creator, director, screenwriter, actor, producer, designer and businessperson.

Best known as the creative mastermind behind Neon Genesis Evangelion, Anno has plenty more strings to his bow, working in live-action and illustration as well as being a voice actor.

Hayao Miyazaki

Japanese manga artist "Mangaka", animator, director, producer, screenwriter and author.

Hayao Miyazaki co-founder of Studio Ghibli, a film and animation studio, he has attained international acclaim as a masterful storyteller and as a maker of animated feature films, and is widely regarded as one of the most accomplished filmmakers in the history of animation.

Masashi Kishimoto

Japanese manga artist "Mangaka"

His best known work, Naruto, has sold over 250 million copies worldwide in 46 countries. The series has been adapted into three anime and multiple films, video games, and related media. Besides the Naruto manga, Kishimoto also personally supervised the two canonical anime films, and has written several one-shot stories.
